# Viral Launch Pack

This folder captures the agreed v1 direction for **Who Approved My Human** and breaks each decision into reviewable docs.

## What This Covers

- Locked 4-dimension type system (`C/Y`, `K/B`, `V/T`, `G/O`) with 16 total combinations
- Viral-first product strategy and scoring model
- Memeable naming system and copy direction
- Social proof module (most common + rarest type)
- Lightweight cross-agent compare mode
- Spicy-mode safety guardrails
- Zero-friction onboarding (no signup first run)
- Visual identity system for reusable character figures (results + share cards)

## Product Promise (v1)

"What does your AI actually think of you?"

Short, playful variant options:

- "Wanna see how your AI reads you?"
- "Your AI has opinions. Ready?"
- "You + your AI = a personality type."

## v1 Success Signals

- High completion rate from first question to results page
- High share-card creation rate
- Good replay loop via "Try another agent"
- Low abandonment before first result
